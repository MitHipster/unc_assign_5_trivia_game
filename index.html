    Instructions

    • Welcome to Trivia Quest! There are six categories and you must get 1 question right from each category to win, but if you answer 3 questions wrong in total, you lose.
    • Categories are selected randomly and once you answer a question you earn that category and move on to the remaining categories.
    • If you answer incorrectly, the category remains in play and can be randomly selected again.
    • You have 30 seconds to answer each question and correct answers are worth 10 points each.
    • If time runs out or you answer incorrectly, you lose 10 points.
    • As a bonus, you receive 1 point for each second that remains on the timer when you answer correctly. Click anywhere to begin.
